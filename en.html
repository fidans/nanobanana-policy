Privacy Policy

App: NanoBanana  •  Last updated: 2025-09-02

Overview

NanoBanana lets you generate images from text. We use Firebase Authentication for sign-in (Apple/Google). We do not sell personal data.

Data We Process

  • Account data: Email or Apple/Google account identifiers via Firebase Auth.
  • Prompts: Text you send to generate images is proxied through our Firebase Cloud Function to Fal. We do not persist prompts or generated images on our servers.
  • Device storage: Images you choose to save are stored locally in Photos (with your permission).

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions.
  • Generate images on demand via our proxy integration with Fal.
  • Show daily quota status.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the app: Firebase (Google) for authentication and Fal for image generation. We do not sell personal data or use third-party advertising SDKs.

Retention

Authentication records are retained by Firebase while your account exists. We do not keep generated images server-side.

Deletion

You can delete your account in the app: Settings → Delete My Account. This removes your Firebase user record. Images saved to Photos remain on your device.

Security

API keys are kept server-side behind a Firebase Cloud Function proxy. All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13.
